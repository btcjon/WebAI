Quick Start Guide
Get your Google Bard Token

Open Google Chrome and navigate to https://bard.google.com/
Press F12 for console
Go to Application → Cookies → __Secure-1PSID. Copy the value of that cookie. That is your bard token.

Update your agent settings

Update BARD_TOKEN with the value of __Secure-1PSID.
AI_MODEL is bard for Google Bard.
